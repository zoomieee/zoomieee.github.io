Low-Power Wide Swing Cascode OTA

  • Designed a differential wide swing cascode OTA in CMOS 50 nm technology.
  • Targeted low power and compact area while delivering 51.1 dB gain and 128.8 MHz UGF.
  • Validated across PVT corners with FOMs between 1175 and 1380.

Switched-Capacitor Biquad Filter for ECG

  • Designed the filter for reliable amplitude and frequency response in biomedical systems.

MCMC Processor with Knuth–Yao Sampler

  • Verified timing, area, and power using Synopsys Design Compiler and VCS.
  • Completed the RTL-to-GDSII flow with placement, routing, and sign-off.

8-bit Threshold Leaky ReLU Circuit

  • Designed an 8-bit TLReLU circuit in TSMC 16 nm CMOS using a carry-select adder architecture.
  • Achieved a total transistor count of 1098 and a propagation delay of 120 ps.

2.4 GHz RF Front-End (LNA + 4-Path Mixer)

  • Designed a common-source inductive degeneration LNA in TSMC 16 nm achieving 18 dB power gain, 1.1 dB noise figure, and 1 mW power at 2.4 GHz (802.11g).
  • Achieved simultaneous noise and input matching via source degeneration and gate matching network optimization (S11).
  • Implemented a 4-path passive mixer with non-overlapping LO phases for low-loss downconversion and harmonic rejection.
  • Validated performance using AC, transient, and noise simulations; evaluated gain, NF, and frequency response.

TSMC 16 nm FinFET Inverter Layout

  • Ensured the full grid remained LVS and DRC clean across the technology library rules.

TSMC 16 nm FinFET 3-Input NAND Layout

  • Completed full 3-input NAND gate layout in TSMC 16 nm FinFET technology with LVS and DRC clean signoff.
  • Implemented diffusion sharing in the NMOS series network and optimized PMOS parallel placement to minimize area and parasitic capacitances.
  • Applied FinFET layout constraints including fin quantization, poly dummy insertion, and well/tap structures in accordance with technology design rules.
  • Verified the layout through LVS and DRC, achieving a clean signoff across the TSMC 16 nm technology rule deck.
